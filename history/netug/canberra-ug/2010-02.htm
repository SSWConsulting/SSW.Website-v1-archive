Canberra .NET User Group

You are cordially invited to the FEBRUARY meetings of the Canberra .NET Developers User Group.

The lunch time meeting is being held on MONDAY 15th February from 12.30pm to 1.30pm.

The evening meeting is being held on MONDAY 15th February from 4.30pm to 6pm.

Agenda:
  • 20 mins - Pizza, Coke and Networking
  • 60 mins - What is Scrum... and is another project methodology really that useful? by John Liu
    While software development has been around for a while, projects and managing them has been around for even longer, so why should development teams be interested in a project management methodology that has been discussed widely only over the last few years? How does it relate to other Agile methodologies and will Scrum help when your team needs to deliver world class software on time and budget?
    Yes, and in this session you will shown how Scrum works with:
    • The important concepts of Scrum and how it relates with other Agile techniques
    • The features of Scrum to make it adaptable to your team, what types of projects it can be applied to and challenges that can arise
    • Plus a couple of things that will maximize your productivity with Scrum in practice
  • 10 mins - Question time
