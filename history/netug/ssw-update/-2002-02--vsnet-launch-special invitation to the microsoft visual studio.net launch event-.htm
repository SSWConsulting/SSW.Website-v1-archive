Events
Visual Studio.NET Launch Event - Wednesday 27th February - 6pm

Adam Cogan and Microsoft's Charles Sterling have arranged for a special invitation to the members of the Sydney .NET User Group to the preview Launch Event of VS.NET.

Coming along will be Tony Goodhew and David Lazar, two Microsoft products managers who were instrumental in the creation of Visual Studio .NET. Learn from them and expand your knowledge of .NET right from the source. If you've got any questions be sure to bring them along.

If that isn't reason enough to be at the Grace Hotel by 6pm, there will be free beer, pizza and T-shirts for all attendees. Why would you miss out?
