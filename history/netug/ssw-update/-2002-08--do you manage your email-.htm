Do you manage your email?
Events
Sydney .NET User Group
Wednesday 21st August
This month Adam Cogan will be talking about the problems of project management and task tracking in an eXtreme world. His focus will be on how you can use Exchange Server as your tool of choice. All developers spent a good part of their life using Outlook and most of the external bugs are reported by email, so it makes sense to write a COM add-in to assist with this problem. COM Add-ins can be written inside VS.NET using COM Interop and he will be showing you one such solution.

Wednesday August 21 2002, 5:45 pm at Microsoft, 1 Epping Road.
