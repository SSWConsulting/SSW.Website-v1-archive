Sportstec’s partnership with the New South Wales Institute of Sport (NSWIS) demonstrates the success of SportsCode, Sportstec’s digital video analysis system.

NSWIS is one of the world’s leading sporting facilities and at the forefront of sports training, sports science and sports medicine. It’s also fully accredited to provide training in the use of SportsCode. NSWIS coaches have already started to use the system in athlete preparation programs for soccer, hockey, basketball, softball, cricket, water polo, netball, diving, cycling, sailing and speed skating.

“SportsCode is a major professional development opportunity for NSWIS coaches,” says John Crampton of NSWIS. “Its value is in its flexibility and speed – and it has rapidly become the international industry standard in game analysis systems.
It means we can look at what actually happens during a competition and make improvements in training or in the next game.”
