Setup

After completing the Log On screen, click "Setup".

Note: If you have previously setup 'SSW Data PRO!' you will proceed to the 'Change' screen.


A confirmatory screen will be displayed. If you want to observe each of the preparatory changes to your front and back ends, choose "Show all messages for change".



This screen will display if you have succeeded.

Note: You only have to go through this process once. To confirm this click on close door button in the left hand corner of the 'Change' form and relogin by clicking 'Next' button on the 'Log On' form.
