Training at UTS

Before you leave to go to UTS, it is important that you speak to Adam to go over what you will be teaching.

You will need to know and do:

  • Where to go and what time
  • Where to find the presentation
  • Where to find the hands on labs
  • The client UTS is to be invoiced for 6pm to 9pm. Any time spent at UTS after 9pm cannot be billed
  • If you want to claim the overtime, it will need to approved the following day
  • Focus on the demos and not the slides
  • Dont prepare at a client
  • Get the ppt and hands-on-lab initialled by Adam
