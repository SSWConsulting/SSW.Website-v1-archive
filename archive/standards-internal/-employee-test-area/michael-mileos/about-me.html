Michael Mileos

  • Introduction

    My name is Michael Mileos, and I'm and eXtreme kind of guy. My favourite activities include skateboarding, rocking out on my guitar, watching movies, clubbing with friends, moshing at concerts, and of course - Developing Software. My taste in music ranges from Hard Rock to Dance to Trance and then to R 'n B.  I also like to travel and have lived in New York, frequented Athens, and have visited London, Canada, Japan, and most recently, The Kingdom of Bahrain (in transit).

  • Education

    In year 8, at Inaburra High School, I encountered my first very first programming language - QBASIC. While I found it interesting, it was not until year 10 that I began using Visual Basic and was truly drawn into programming. I made many fun little programs, ranging from Joke Applications to an Internet Chat Program with basic encryption, and this was just for fun in my spare time. I also enjoyed making homepages, and had designed a fan site for my favorite music artist, at the time, Tupac Shakur. I took 3U Computing Studies and graduated high school in 1998.

    I then had an opportunity to complete higher education in New York, and so.. I took it! While studying at the City University of New York's College of Staten Island ( CSI ), I completed two degrees: An Associate in Applied Science (A.A.S.) in Computer Programming, and a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science. I completed both of these degrees with honors, and as well as being on the Dean's list, I also received scholarships for most of my years (after becoming a New York resident). My studies at CSI saw me spending most of my time in C++; however, we used some VB and Java as well.

  • Future Aspirations

    Who knows what the future brings? In the future I aspire to be "Happy." Ideally I would like to be developing software at a higher level, and making lots of money would be good too.
